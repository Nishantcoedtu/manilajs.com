The first JavaScript meetup in the Philippines, ever! Join us for an evening of JavaScript talks, networking, and camaraderie. Beginners and experts welcome!

Jason Torres Proudcloud

A state of the nation on JavaScript — Jason shares what he knows about JavaScript's past, where it is now, and where it's headed to the future.

James Florentino Wishbone Media

Getting started with JavaScript for Flash programmers — James tells us how JavaScript and ActionScript differs and what pitfalls to look out for when doing JS.

Rico Sta. Cruz Nadarei, Inc.

Why you should consider JavaScript as a server-side language — Rico shares his experiences on building apps with Node.js.
