Join us for an evening of JavaScript talks, networking, and camaraderie. Beginners and experts welcome!

Joko Sarmiento

Joko is the author of the wildly-popular framework Fries, used to to prototype Android interfaces in HTML. Joko will be sharing with us his experiences in building an HTML prototyping tool.
